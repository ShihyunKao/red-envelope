# Cyber Fortune Toss

**Date:** 8 February 2026  
**Author:** Shihyun Kao  

## Instructions (operation manual)
This is a cross-device, real-time interactive experience.
1. Open the display link on a computer monitor or projector.
2. Open the mobile controller link on a smartphone.
3. On the mobile device, tap the "TAP TO START" button to grant device motion and orientation permissions.
4. Grip your phone securely and perform a "tossing" or "shaking" gesture. Watch as your physical movements trigger a physics-based explosion of digital red envelopes and coins on the main display!

## Blurb
"Cyber Fortune Toss" is a networked interactive installation that modernizes the traditional Chinese New Year custom of giving red envelopes (Hongbao). Utilizing mobile device accelerometers and WebSockets, the project translates physical throwing gestures into a generative, physics-based visual spectacle on a shared display. By transforming the mobile phone into a performative controller, the work explores the intersection of cultural rituals, networked communication, and embodied interaction, turning a simple screen into a chaotic, joyous celebration of data and movement.
